# Influencer's Hub - Stitch Generation Prompt

**Objective:**
Design a premium, dynamic, and high-conversion landing page for "Influencer's Hub," a double-sided marketplace connecting brands with content creators. The page should feel startup-grade, modern, and trust-inspiring, utilizing micro-interactions, sleek gradients, and clean typography.

**Design System & Aesthetics:**
- **Primary Colors:** Deep Indigo (`#4f46e5`) and Vibrant Fuchsia (`#d946ef`).
- **Backgrounds:** Combinations of crisp white, soft slate gray, soft indigo blurs for depth, and premium black for contrast sections.
- **Typography:** A highly readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans). Use heavy weights (e.g., Extrabold) for main headings and tight tracking for a tech-startup feel.
- **Micro-interactions:** Hover lifts/scales for cards, fade-in text, spring animations on grids. Glassmorphism hints for floating visual elements.
- **Borders & Radiuses:** Use rounded corners (16px to 24px) for cards, sections, and buttons.

**Page Structure & Components:**

### 1. Hero Section
- **Background:** A tall section with a subtle, radial gradient background (from white to soft indigo). Include some floating decorative abstract shapes or "glassmorphic" tags indicating speed ("Fastest Payouts" tag).
- **Typography:** Large, center-aligned headline: "Connect with the Skilled Content Creators At Any Time". Highlight "Connect" in indigo and "At Any Time" in vibrant fuchsia.
- **Subtitle:** "The ultimate platform for high-performance influencer marketing. Post campaigns, discover creators, and scale your brand globally." in soft slate gray.
- **Action Buttons:** A horizontal row containing:
  - "I'm a Brand" with a right arrow icon (Primary solid button).
  - "I'm a Creator" (Secondary button, softer background/outline).

### 2. Stats Banner
- **Layout:** A sleek, edge-to-edge dark/black banner spanning the screen width.
- **Content:** 4 data points separated by subtle dividers.
  - "2,400+" / VERIFIED CREATORS
  - "850+" / ACTIVE CAMPAIGNS
  - "₹12Cr+" / DEALS CLOSED
  - "98%" / SATISFACTION RATE
- **Style:** Numbers should be large, bold, and white. Labels should be small, muted, uppercase, and have wide letter spacing.

### 3. "How It Works" Section
- **Header:** "How It Works" with a light subtitle: "A transparent, two-sided process designed for trust and results."
- **Layout:** A grid of 4 step cards.
- **Cards:**
  - Soft gradient backgrounds (fuchsia to white to indigo).
  - Contain an icon (Target, Users, CheckCircle, Zap) set inside a colored rounded-box.
  - A small "STEP X" pill badge.
  - Bold Title (e.g., "Brands Post Campaigns", "Creators Apply", "2-Step Approval", "Collaborate & Grow").
  - Short descriptive text.
- **Hover Effect:** Ensure these cards feature a prominent "lift" and slight rotation when hovered.

### 4. Featured Campaigns
- **Container:** A soft slate/gray padded section with heavily rounded corners.
- **Header Structure:** Title ("Featured Campaigns") and subtitle on the left, with a "View all" textual link and arrow aligned to the right.
- **Cards (Grid of 3):**
  - Crisp white background.
  - **Header:** Brand initial in a square box + Campaign Title + Brand Name.
  - **Tag:** Solid tinted pill badge indicating the Niche (e.g., "Tech", "Beauty").
  - **Description:** 2 lines of description text.
  - **Footer:** Divided by top border. Features the Budget (e.g., "₹50,000" in primary bold text) and an "active" status pill (green text on light green background).

### 5. Top Performers (Featured Creators)
- **Container:** Similar to the campaigns, but with a soft vertical gradient background (fade from soft indigo to white to indigo).
- **Header Structure:** Title ("Top Performers") on the left, "Discover creators" link on the right.
- **Cards (Grid of 3):**
  - Centered layout.
  - Large circular avatar placeholder with an overlapping "verified" green checkmark badge in the bottom right corner.
  - Creator Name (Bold, 20px).
  - Niche Tag (e.g., "Gaming" or "Fitness").
  - Short Bio text.
  - A full-width "View Profile" button spanning the bottom of the card.

### 6. Final Call-to-Action (CTA)
- **Container:** A wide card with a prominent Primary Indigo background. Use blurred, colorful overlay circles (fuchsia/light indigo) near the corners to provide glow and depth.
- **Content:** Center aligned. Include a "TrendingUp" icon.
- **Copy:** "Ready to Scale Your Brand?" as a large bold white/light header. 
- **Buttons:** 
  - "Get Started Free" (Solid white background, indigo text).
  - "Browse Campaigns" (Transparent background, white border, white text).

**Notes for AI Generation:** 
- Keep the design highly visual. Do not rely heavily on massive blocks of text.
- Fully populate the screen with accurate, realistic dummy data for brands and creators.
- Prioritize responsiveness—grid sizes must shrink to 1 column on mobile.
